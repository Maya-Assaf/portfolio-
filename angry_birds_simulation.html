Angry Birds Simulation

An immersive 3D simulation of the renowned Angry Birds game, meticulously crafted using the Unity engine from the ground up, while eschewing Unity's built-in physics functionalities.

This simulation boasts comprehensive impact modeling, precise impact detection, structural fracture, explosive interactions, dynamic deformation, projectile pulling, and strategic bird tossing, all complemented by accurate trajectory predictions for avian projectiles.

In this simulation, an array of avian characters is faithfully replicated, encompassing the classic normal bird, the formidable heavyweight bird, split bird, explosive bird, and the high-velocity bird.  

with simulation glass, wood, and iron. This intricate material simulation plays a pivotal role in simulating the destruction of structures and the consequential elimination of mischievous pigs, where the level of damage inflicted is proportionate to the applied force.

Our simulation is characterized by its limitless creative potential, allowing players to construct diverse and imaginative buildings to challenge their strategic prowess, catering to the desires of the player community. With meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to an authentic Angry Birds experience, our 3D simulation offers a captivating and dynamic gaming environment that remains true to the spirit of the original.
