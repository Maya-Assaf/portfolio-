Pharmaline

PharmalineÂ  is a mobile application represents a cutting-edge platform dedicated to the seamless electronic sale of medical products.

Users have the privilege of registering and logging in to establish an active presence within the Pharmaline community.

Once onboard, a wide array of functionalities becomes accessible, empowering users in various capacities.

As a user, you can effortlessly list products for sale while simultaneously browsing and purchasing items.

Moreover, you retain full control over your personal account, allowing for convenient modifications and a comprehensive view of the available product offerings.

Interactivity within our platform is enriched through features such as product reviews, likes, and direct communication with both fellow customers and sellers.

You possess the unique ability to both initiate and respond to offers, assuming the dual roles of seller and buyer.

Our search functionality is robust, enabling the pinpoint location of specific products and the organization of search history.

Additionally, an automated product expiry schedule streamlines the process of managing product listings, including the addition, deletion, and modification of items.

When exploring user profiles, you gain access to a comprehensive view of their product listings and can engage in direct communication through messaging, likes, and comments. Furthermore, detailed product descriptions facilitate informed browsing and selection.

In essence, our mobile application redefines the medical product procurement experience, offering a multifaceted platform that caters to both sellers and buyers while fostering a vibrant and connected community.
