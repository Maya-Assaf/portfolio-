YUGO

Description: YUGO is Mobile application designed to connect passengers with nearby taxi drivers.


  SERVICES PROVIDED BY YUGO:

- Account Creation: Our service facilitates users (customers and drivers alike) in establishing a personal account, enabling them to seamlessly integrate into the esteemed YUGO community.

- Login: Users can access our application securely through email or phone number authentication, ensuring a protected and personalized experience.

- Order Fulfillment: Users have the liberty to select the most suitable mode of transportation from our diverse fleet, catering to their unique requirements.

- Customized Service Requests: Our application empowers users to place bespoke service requests with YUGO's contracted drivers, tailoring their experience to specific needs and preferences.

- Profile Management: Users can meticulously curate their profiles, ensuring accuracy and relevance in their interactions within the YUGO community.

- Vehicle Selection: We offer users the ability to choose their desired vehicle, complete with customizable features, ensuring a tailored and comfortable journey.

- Complaint Submission: In the unfortunate event of an unpleasant experience, users can lodge complaints with our company, ensuring swift resolution and enhanced customer satisfaction.

- Lost Item Reporting: Our platform allows users to report lost items, providing detailed information to facilitate their recovery, ensuring a hassle-free resolution process.

- Car Sharing Partnerships: To enhance safety and affordability, users can find compatible car-sharing partners, promoting secure travel and cost-sharing opportunities.

- Favorite Locations: Users can save preferred locations within the application, streamlining service requests and optimizing time management for their utmost convenience.

- Optimized Transportation: We prioritize efficiency by connecting users with the nearest private transportation options, ensuring swift and timely travel, irrespective of other alternatives.

- Cost-Effective Travel: Our commitment to affordability ensures users access the most economical transportation choices available, prioritizing financial prudence without compromising quality service.

- Goods Transportation: We offer reliable and secure transport solutions for goods, facilitating seamless logistics for businesses and individuals alike.

- Disability Services: YUGO provides additional services tailored for individuals with disabilities, ensuring accessibility and comfort in every journey undertaken.

- Child-Friendly Services: Our specialized services cater to families with children, offering safety features and comfort, ensuring a stress-free travel experience for parents and their little ones.

- Smoking-Friendly Services: For our smoking customers, we offer dedicated services, accommodating their preferences and ensuring a comfortable environment throughout their journey.

We are dedicated to providing an unparalleled and tailored service experience for all our users, prioritizing convenience, safety, and satisfaction.
